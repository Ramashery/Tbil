Our Hostel

Hostel "Tbilhome" is located in the historical center of Tbilisi, a 2-minute walk from the famous sulfur baths of the Abanotubani district, the Fig Gorge, and in close proximity to the Kura River embankment.

This is exactly that part of Old Tbilisi, from where all the main sights of the city are easily within walking distance: Rike Park with a cable car to Europe Square, the Narikala Fortress, and Freedom Square.

Hostel "Tbilhome" has 34 beds across different room types to suit every traveler's needs and budget.
